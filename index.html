Registration


Every accepted article should be registered by at least one author to appear in the proceedings. The acceptance list of articles will be announced on the website and the authors will be intimated regarding the acceptance through e-mail. The details about events and dates of registration are given below:


Registration and Camera-ready submission of the accepted articles 01 Feb 2023
Author registration with late fees of INR 1600 (Indian authors) or $30 (foreign authors) 02 Feb – 15 Feb 2023

Registration includes ALL the following events to be completed by the author’s aforementioned deadline.


Registration fee details


Author Registration:

To ensure the publication of a paper in the Proceedings, at least one author has to register by submitting a normal registration fee within the deadline as indicated below.


Note: All bank charges must be borne by the payer.


The normal registration fee covers publication charges and lunch. The over-length charges beyond 10 pages (in LNNS format) will be added to the Normal registration fees to calculate the actual registration fee to be paid by the authors. The over-length charge is Rs. 800 (for Indian authors) or $15 (for foreign authors) per extra page for a maximum of four extra pages.


Listener Registration:


Listeners can register to attend the conference by registering on or before 30th November 2021 12:00 hrs IST. Listener registration includes the following events to be completed by the author within the aforementioned deadline.

  2. Direct Transfer

Cancellation of Registration


If any author/listener wants to cancel his or her registration, he must write a mail to icacie2022@gmail.com on or before 25-NOV-2022 with the subject “Cancellation of Registration – ICACIE XXX”. A processing fee of INR 2500 will be deducted from the paid registration fee and the rest amount will be returned to the author/listener on 5-DEC-2022


No request for authorship cancellation can be made after 25-NOV-2022.
